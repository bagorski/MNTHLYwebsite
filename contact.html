Application Issues

If you are experiencing issues with MNTHLY, you can view the application logs by following these steps:

  1. Open the Settings tab in the MNTHLY app.
  2. Scroll down and tap on Application Logs.
  3. Tap a log file to preview it.
  4. Tap on the Share button in the top right corner to save the log.

Then, attach the log to your support email.

Try to describe the issue you are experiencing in detail and include any steps you took to reproduce the issue.
