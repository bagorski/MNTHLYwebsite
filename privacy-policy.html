Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MNTHLY handles your data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Personal Information: MNTHLY does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Usage Data: MNTHLY does not collect data on how you use the app.

Location Data: MNTHLY does not request or collect location data.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: MNTHLY does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

2. Data Storage

All user data entered into MNTHLY is stored in iCloud, managed by Apple. We do not have access to this data. For information on how Apple handles your data, please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

3. Data Use and Sharing

Since MNTHLY does not collect or store user data, we do not use or share any data. All data is managed by Apple in iCloud.

4. User Rights

Data Access and Deletion: You can manage your data directly within MNTHLY. The app provides options to delete all stored data and download a copy of the data you have entered. For additional details on managing your iCloud data, please refer to Apple’s Privacy Resources.

5. Security

As MNTHLY does not handle user data directly, Apple is responsible for data security related to iCloud storage. Please refer to Apple’s security practices for more information.

6. Purpose and Disclaimer

MNTHLY is designed to help you track bills and expenses and send reminders about upcoming payments. However, it is your responsibility to ensure that all payments are made on time. MNTHLY is not liable for any damages or issues that arise from missed payments or any errors in tracking. Always verify your payment obligations independently.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on our official website at mnthly.app. Please check periodically for updates.

8. Legal Compliance

MNTHLY complies with relevant privacy laws, but since we do not handle data directly, we defer to Apple’s policies and practices for legal compliance.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mnthly.app.
